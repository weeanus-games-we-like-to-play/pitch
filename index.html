game dESIGN

the game will be a series of interconnected screens where

ounces of sought after video gratification enjoyment will come from exploring the wonderful world of WEEANUS games we like to play

the game world environment presents you in stunning some-definition claritiy

as the player explores the world , more options to better explore the world will open up

tight challenging "br00tal" platforming will be expected from the player in exchange for this privilege

and even the most skilled and dedicated of players

will be >on the tips of their tipsy toes

oh and youll only have a certain amount of lives before you have to start over from

the designated save rest stops peppered across the game world for the players convenience

players will be trained into believing that their only goal is to collect all the items that you can collect for a special reward

meet like minded individuals who share your interests in your game area

work your way up to the top of the simulation ladder and prove you still got it

GRAPHICS AND SOUND

the visual audio presentation will be in 2 dimensional stereoeoeoeo

describing the visual style would take away from the immersion

a suRreal clash of mish mash should stand on its own

really rudimentary stuff, just need some sprites and we'll be good to go

the user interface will contain only what is necessary, a lives count and a item count

a dialogue system will need to be made to allow players to converse with individuals who really have something to say

the effects will be all bits and bloops and extracted from nature recordings

the music will be composed by Me and will sound like it belongs

artificial intelligence

the artificial intelligence will be simple

enemies and harzards will follow pre determined paths and whatnots within each screen of the game

and perhaps more complex enemies could be programmed into the game but thatd be ... devilish

PHYSICs

no physics will be needed for this game except for the gravity that

affects the player dragging them down if the player chooses to defy it

adding slight momentum to the players movement would not be a half-bad idea either

there will have to be obligatory ice blocks that are slippery too, cant EVER forget those

MODULES

Class: Screen

the screen class will hold all of the modificatable values that a screen could possibly have like gravity and the name of the screen

Class: Block

the block class will be the meta class that will define the behavior blocks will have and what the block will look like

Class: Player

the player class will contain all the necessary values that will need to be tracked like health and number of items collected

the player class will need methods that will interface with the enemy and item classes

Class: Enemy

the enemy class will be the meta class that will define the behavior for enemies and contain all the necessary values like damage and move speed

the enemy class will have to have methods in place that will interface with the players health values

Class: Item

the item class will really just be an glorified block that will have a method that interfaces with the player to add to their item count

group

a smaller group would be more suited towards the game

more focus will be put towards the feel and map design of the game as

the game play will be decidely more simple
